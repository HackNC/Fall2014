What is HackNC?

HackNC is a place for university and high school students alike to come together and create big things in a small amount of time - specifically 24 hours. The event is typically a test of skill, teamwork and time management for those who know their way around the code to create anything from websites to games to hardware. In the end, the best projects are awarded prizes!

But don’t worry, HackNC is not just for the advanced programmer, the event also aims toward educating those less familiar with the “ints” and outs. Guest lectures, hands-on demos, and tutorials from sponsors, experts, and professors that range from introductory concepts to the latest groundbreaking research will be key focuses during the event in hopes of inspiring the curious and the creative no matter the level of expertise.

FAQs

Who can attend? High school, undergraduate, and graduate students are welcome to join. But others will gladly be welcomed to come check out the cool projects.
What's going to happen? A 24 hour period of hacking during which you will become a better programmer, meet new people, and make cool stuff. You'll have the chance to show off and win prizes!
Do I sign up as an individual or a team? Sign up as an individual! You can create your team whenever and with whoever you like.
What can I hack? HackNC is both a software and hardware hackathon. Code for web, mobile, desktop, and hack away at hardware to your heart's content.
Can we build off existing code? The purpose of the event is to see what you can do in the time you're given, but there is no strict law against existing code.
Will there be food? Hackathon essentaily means programming, caffeinated students, and endless amounts of food.
